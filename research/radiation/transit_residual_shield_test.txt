INTERPRETATION
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If the spec anti-rad shield removes a radiation component on TRANSIT, no-shield residual should exceed shield residual
(diff > 0), and diff ≈ 0.70 × radiation. If diff ≈ 0 (or negative), no shieldable radiation is present on TRANSIT.
Residuals are dominated by the meteor formula's ~9% systematic under-prediction, so expect a positive residual in BOTH
states; the SHIELD TEST is whether no-shield − shield differs, not whether residual is nonzero.
