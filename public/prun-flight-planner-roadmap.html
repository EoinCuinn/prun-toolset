RADIATION damage formula Next arc
Formula confirmed by [OOG] Aem | SR via KQ-451 BTF runs (O-type system). With advanced anti-rad shielding: %/Mkm = 0.0224 × AU⁻². Without shielding: %/Mkm = 0.0734 × AU⁻². Unavoidable floor ~0.0002%/Mkm independent of distance. Reduction factor ~70% for advanced anti-rad. Relevant for O/B star systems and inner-planet routes. Not yet implemented in planner. Source: Damage_plotting.xlsx, KQ-451 sheet.
