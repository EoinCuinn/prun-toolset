TO fuel formula Fixed — 2026-07-12
Root cause: STL fuel flow rate was baked into the constant K=5.05, assuming the Standard STL Engine (0.015 units/sec). All validation ships used Standard — bug was invisible. Fuel-saving STL Engine (0.0075) exposed the 2× error. Fix: explicit flowRate parameter in atmLeg(), normalised against 0.015. Commit f179291. AVI-072QE confirmed exact: planner 25.0 vs game 25 ✓. TO/LND fuel — engine cross-check Confirmed 4/5 — 2026-07-14

LDG fuel — engine-physics model Solved & implemented — 2026-07-14
Landing fuel is the same STL burn physics as take-off: fuel = k × flow × √(dist/accel), k≈362 (≈340 vs loaded-mass accel). Engine-dependent via BOTH flow rate and acceleration — this refutes the earlier "engine-independent / per-blueprint / copy-carries-old-mechanics" theories, which were the flow+accel signal misread from uncontrolled comparisons (different engines never isolated). Proven on a controlled 5-engine set (identical build, only the STL engine swapped), 4 of 5 engines to ±1.5% across a 6× distance range and 4 planets. Planner now computes landing fuel through the same atmLeg physics as take-off — √(dist/(13.71·accel/350.11)) × flow/0.015 — replacing the old flat constant; reproduces 4/5 engines to ~1%. ⚠ Hyperthrust reads ~21% low until its outlier (k≈439) is solved. (Planner change is in the working tree, pending commit.)
LND distance formula — rewritten 2026-07-14 Done — 2026-07-14
Root cause: landing distance is driven by a randomly-rolled approach orbit (semiMajorAxis), rerolled on every blueprint tester window reset. Engine, time, and planet position are all irrelevant. Universal formula: landing_dist_km = (20/3) × pressureFactor × (sma − radius) / 1000. Mean estimate used in planner = planet_term = radius_km × pressureFactor. Band: ±15%. Universal constant C=20/3 confirmed across Promitor (P=1.007), Beanironia (P=29.6), Odysseus (P=0.693) — 45 samples, 3 planets, 43× pressure range (0.69→29.6 atm), 2.7× radius range. All per-engine ENGINE_LANDING_RATIO table and intra/cross-system regime logic removed. Commit 34ef374. Credit: Taiyi Bureau — approach orbit / window-reset theory.
